Privacy Policy for MyDompet

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: MyDompet is committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

MyDompet collects the following types of information to provide you with a personalized financial management experience:

1.1 Account Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Profile Data: Name, preferred currency, and other optional profile details

1.2 Financial Data

  • Transaction Records: Income and expense entries you create
  • Categories: Expense and income categories you define
  • Payment Methods: Names of payment methods (e.g., "Cash", "Credit Card")
  • Account Balances: Financial totals calculated from your transactions

1.3 Usage Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Analytics: App usage patterns to improve user experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide core financial tracking and management features
  • Data Synchronization: To sync your data across your devices
  • Analytics & Insights: To generate financial reports and insights for you
  • App Improvement: To identify bugs and improve functionality
  • Security: To protect your account from unauthorized access

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a cloud-based PostgreSQL database with enterprise-grade security:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Data Isolation: Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure you can only access your own data
  • Server Location: Data is stored in secure data centers with 99.9% uptime

3.2 Security Measures

  • Authentication: Secure email/password authentication with password hashing
  • Access Control: Only authenticated users can access their own financial data
  • Regular Backups: Automated backups to prevent data loss
  • Secure Token Storage: Authentication tokens stored using device hardware encryption

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We DO NOT sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties.

The only third-party services we use are:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication provider (see their Privacy Policy)
  • Expo/React Native: Mobile app framework (no data collection by default)

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time
  • Modification: Edit or update your transactions and profile information
  • Deletion: Delete individual transactions or your entire account
  • Export: Request a copy of your data (contact us)
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics or stop using the app at any time

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Account Deletion: When you delete your account, all data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backups: Backup copies are retained for 90 days for disaster recovery purposes

7. Children's Privacy

MyDompet is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

8. International Users

MyDompet is available globally. By using the app, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of your country of residence.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Significant changes will be announced via in-app notification or email
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

11. EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

  • App Name: MyDompet
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
